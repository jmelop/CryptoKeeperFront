First open source app that allow to save your crypto data and check the market in real live. Check the most important Twitter profiles, crypto fluctuations or the news of the most important websites.

Advantages

A better way to control your cryptocurrency

If you have your cryptocurrency in multiple exchanges or you are using multiple wallets this is your app. You will be able to upload your transactions information to track in just single app/web.

Full Privacy

We don't save emails or any kind of information to track you. We will generate to you one username and random password of 12 words to login.

Download the app in your local server

You can use our cloud services or you could download the app in your local server and you won't lose any functionality.

Wallet and social tracker

We provide you the live analysis of the most important crypto wallets, most famous and influencing Twitter profiles and the most important news in cryptocurrency world.

Mobile and email notifications

You can choose when you would like to receive a notification. If the price of a cryptocurrency go down or one of the biggest wallet do a movement you will be notify.
